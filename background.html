Proposed Project

Our proposed solution is organizing a project intervention throughout the madrasah, which will give us the opportunity to reduce waste that clogs the sewage and drainage systems and to purchase recycling materials, such as bins, shovels, and sanitary materials. in order to dispose of the waste properly and repair other damages caused by the flooding.
